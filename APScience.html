AP Earth and Environmental :

This class is one that tends to get mixed reviews as some people think the exam is easy and others think its difficult. The test has 80 multiple choice questions (for the 2020 exam) and 3 free response questions (design an investigation, analyze an environmental problem and propose a solution, analyze an environmental problem and propose a solution doing calculations). This class is a great beginner into AP Science classes, but I did find it difficult especially in the beginning. The study techniques listed below are some great ways to get yourself prepared for this exam.

Review Books

Barron's AP Environmental Science, 5th Edition by Gary S.Thorpe

This book has great recommendations if you take AP Environmental Science.
It is said to help give you a five on the AP exam and and has great practice test.
The 2 full length tests are great and will prepare you for questions that you will
see on the AP exam. The books provides lots of information and defines and
explain vocab terms that you will need to understand the questions on the final.
I have not personally used this book but it has been recommended to me by people
who have said that Barron's AP Environmental Science is very helpful and it is the
eason they have gotten a five.
