About Us!

Our team is made up of four young women whose main mission is to make resources and preparation information for AP students more accessible and easier to understand. Our names are Anusha Samal, Adji Mbacke, Najah Williams, and Zoey McCrimmon.

When were we established?

We became established on August 5, 2019 and ever since then our passion of excelling in school and our experience with taking AP classes gives us motivation everyday.
